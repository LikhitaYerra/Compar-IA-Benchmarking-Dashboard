Why this is novel

Compar'IA differs from quality-only LLM leaderboards by coupling usefulness with environmental and operational cost.
  • One interface for quality, latency, energy, CO₂, and cost.
  • Normalized scoring that excludes invalid or zero denominators instead of producing infinite rankings.
  • Extensible to new LLMs, water usage, regional carbon intensity, or memory footprint as additional CSV columns.
  • Exportable evidence trail for papers, internal procurement, and policy reports.

Extending the dashboard

Compar'IA was designed so non-experts can extend it.
  1. Add a row in data_collection_template.csv for each new model and task.
  2. Optionally add new columns for additional sustainability indicators.
  3. Reload the dashboard; aggregations, scoring, and recommendations recompute automatically.
